About Kottayam

Kottayam is a city in the Indian state of Kerala. It is located in central Kerala and is also the administrative capital of the Kottayam district. The city is known for its picturesque landscapes, serene backwaters, and rich cultural heritage.

Places to Visit

  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary: A paradise for bird watchers, located on the banks of Vembanad Lake.
  • Vembanad Lake: The longest lake in India and the largest lake in Kerala.
  • Thirunakkara Mahadeva Temple: A historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Elaveezha Poonchira: A beautiful hill station offering panoramic views of the surrounding valleys.

Culture and Traditions

Kottayam is known for its rich cultural heritage. The town is famous for its traditional festivals, classical dance forms like Kathakali, and its contribution to Malayalam literature. The annual Kottayam Fest is a major cultural event that attracts visitors from all over the state.
